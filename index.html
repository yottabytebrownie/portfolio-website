About

Dr. Soumyashree Kar is an Assistant Professor at IIT Bombay, a role she has held since January 2025. Prior to this, she worked as a research scientist at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and served as a data scientist at the University of Southern Mississippi from January 2022 to February 2024.

Dr. Kar completed her postdoctoral research at Iowa State University, following her time as a Research Scholar and Teaching Assistant at IIT Bombay between January 2016 and April 2021. During this period, she also participated in a student exchange program at the University of Tokyo.

Earlier in her career, Dr. Kar held a Senior Research Fellowship at the Indian Agricultural Research Institute (August to November 2015), and completed a research internship at the Odisha Space Applications Centre. She has also worked as a software engineer at HSBC
